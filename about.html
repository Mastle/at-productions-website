My Career in a Short Rertrospect

I originally began my career as a language teacher. Exploring the technical intricacies of language and concepts like syntax and semantics through linguistics sparked my curiosity about how these ideas intersect with computer science.

This curiosity led me to create my own language teaching platform, where I had my first hands-on experience building a website using a content management system. After attending a brief C++ bootcamp and experimenting with C#, I started helping clients get their small-scale apps up and running in C#.

A turning point came when a friend enlisted my help to create a website for his startup using another CMS. That project made me realize I had a knack for web development. Around the same time, I joined a travel agency as a developer, where I wrote several scripts in UiPath to automate their tasks.

My next adventure was teaching a six-month extracurricular course on basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to high school students. Watching them grow and gain confidence in coding was incredibly fulfilling, and it confirmed that frontend web development is where I truly belong.

Today, my primary focus is creating apps and websites for clients through ATproductions. I also explore my passion for linguistics and language education through Joyful Speaking, a platform I hold close to my heart.

Stay tuned for the awesome apps I’m currently building and the digital ventures I’m excited to share on my blog here!
