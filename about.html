My Career in a Short Rertrospect

I originally began my career as a language teacher. Exploring the technical intricacies of language and concepts like syntax and semantics through linguistics sparked my curiosity about how these ideas intersect with computer science.

This curiosity led me to create my own language teaching platform, where I had my first hands-on experience building a website using a content management system. A couple of changes required some coding to be done, and that's where this journey began
